About Us

The Global M&A Advisory is an independent boutique transaction advisory firm, serving a global clientele, for Lower Middle Market Deals. The Lower Middle Market spectrum includes both the so‑called Lower Market – Mainstreet ticket sizes, that are typically handled by Business Brokers and the so‑called Middle Market ticket sizes, typically handled by M&A Advisors.

Therefore, with Sector Agnostic competency, we serve both ends of the Lower Middle Market. In addition, we are connected across thousands of strategic/financial sponsors, as well as, businesses, across sectors and industries, meaning we can effectively initiate deal making.

Please note that we work with a few selected clients at a time, in order to preserve the quality of service provided. To continue, preference is given to our long-term recurring clients.

Buying & Selling

We are mandated for target identification and we responsibly execute the entire M&A process. Only short‑listed targets are presented that fit the criteria set out by our clients. Confidentiality is ensured from start to end.

Entrepreneurs spend years building their companies. Once mature, founders deserve to exit and capitalize on their multi‑year hard work by selling to the right buyer – at the right price.

Exit Planning & Valuation

We believe in long‑term, trust‑based relationships that are mutually beneficial. You can contact us free of charge regarding Exit Planning and Valuation.

No suprisses come to the prepared and as such, we can plan years ahead of the time to mandate us for a sell-side advisory engagement.

Our Fees

We are remunerated by the acquirer and as such, there are no fees charged to sellers. We do not charge a monthly retainer fee — only a final transaction success fee, determined by the complexity of the case handled.

There is no time limit on the service we provide along the transaction journey.
