About Us

The Global M&A Advisory is an independent boutique transaction advisory firm, serving a global clientele, for Lower Middle Market business transactions.

The Lower Middle Market spectrum starts with the so‑called Lower Market – Mainstreet Businesses, typically handled by Business Brokers, while Middle Market firms are typically handled by M&A Advisors.

We are connected across thousands of strategic and financial sponsors, meaning finding a relevant investor is no concern. We work with a few selected clients at a time to preserve the quality of service provided.
