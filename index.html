About Us

The Global M&A Advisory is an independent boutique transaction advisory firm, specialised in supporting clients across Global Lower Middle Market Asset and Corporate Deal Structures, involving Investment Property Ownership Transfers. To elaborate, the typical value range for transactions handled is around $5m-$250m and we have a sector-agnostic apporach.

Moving on, we support transaction participants through complex cross-border processes by facilitating coordination, commercial communication, transaction progression, and execution support across multiple jurisdictions.

Our approach is discreet, relationship-driven, and execution-focused. Kindly note that we work with select few clients at a time in order to ensure the provision of a quality service.

Buying & Selling

We are mandated by both Buyers and Sellers and we responsibly execute the entire M&A process. Corporate transaction support and ownership-transfer coordination for successful deal completion is our competency. As such, rest assured that confidentiality is ensured from start to end.

Property Entrepreneurs spend years building their companies. Once mature, founders deserve to exit and capitalize on their multi‑year hard work by selling to the right buyer – at the right price. We will be there assisting both parties in each step of the process.

Exit Planning & Valuation

We believe in long‑term, trust‑based relationships, that are mutually beneficial. You can contact us free of charge regarding Exit Planning and Valuation.

No suprisses come to the prepared and as such, we can plan years ahead of the time you'll mandate us for a sell-side support engagement.

Our Fees

We are remunerated by the acquirer and as such, there are no fees charged to sellers. We do not charge a monthly retainer fee — we believe in a value/outcome-based service and therefore, only a final transaction success fee exists, determined by the complexity of the case handled.

There is no time limit on the service we provide along the transaction journey.
