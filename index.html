About Us

The Global M&A Advisory is an independent boutique transaction advisory firm, serving a global clientele, for Lower Middle Market Deals. The Lower Middle Market spectrum includes both the so‑called Lower Market – Mainstreet ticket sizes, that are typically handled by Business Brokers and the so-called Middle Market ticket sizes, typically handled by M&A Advisors.

Therefore, with Sector Agnostic competency, we serve both ends of the Lower Middle Market. In addition, we are connected across thousands of strategic/financial sponsors, as well as, businesses, across sectors and industries, meaning we can effectively initiate deal making.

Please note that we work with few selected clients at a time, in order to preserve the quality of the service provided. To continue, preference is given to our long-term recurring clients.

Buying

We are mandated for target identification and we responsibly execute the entire M&A process. Only short‑listed targets are presented that fit the criteria set out by our clients. Confidentiality is ensured from start to end.

Selling

Asset Owners and Entrepreneurs spend years in the dark while their holdings are being built towards something of value. Once shiny and mature assets and businesses have been built, owners/founders deserve to exit and capitalize their multi-year hard work, via selling to the right buyer – at the right price. We are experts of the art and we deliver accordingly.

Our Fees

Kindly be informed that as a firm, we are remunerated by the acquirer and as such, there are no fees charged to sellers. We believe that a service should be delivered in a manner that is outcome-based & value-oriented, therefore, we do not charge the typical monthly retainer fee, but only, a final transaction success fee. The transaction success fee will be determined based on the complexity of the case handled. We are acting with integrity and we believe you should be informed about our innovative approach, prior to mandating us. There is no time limit on the service we provide along the transaction journey and you can rest assured we will be there every step of the way.
